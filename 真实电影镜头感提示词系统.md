# 真实电影镜头感提示词系统

> 核心目标：让画面真正像电影，不只是低饱和。重点是 **有限色盘、柔高光、低对比暗部、空气介质、人物缩小和空间关系**。

---

## 1. 核心电影质感母提示词

```text
A true cinematic film still, not a digital concept art render. Shot like a real scene from a high-end arthouse sci-fi film. Limited color palette, soft controlled highlights, low-contrast shadows, rich but restrained dark tones, visible atmospheric haze, air particles catching light, subtle volumetric mist, realistic lens falloff, shallow depth of field, soft defocused edges, natural cinematic bokeh, anamorphic lens character, gentle halation around bright areas, subtle film grain, realistic exposure, no over-sharpening.

The human figure is small within a vast architectural or natural space, emphasizing scale, silence and spatial tension. The environment should feel physically real, with tactile materials, imperfect surfaces, wet reflections, stone, glass, metal, fabric, dust and moisture in the air. Composition uses negative space, off-center framing, layered foreground, middle ground and background, restrained blocking, quiet emotional distance.

Lighting should feel motivated and cinematic: one dominant soft key source, dim ambient fill, gentle rim light, warm highlights against cool shadows, soft gradients, no harsh digital contrast, no flat lighting, no neon overload. The image should feel like a paused frame from a real movie, not an AI illustration.
```

## 2. 中文版准确提示词

```text
真实电影剧照质感，不是数字概念图，不是AI插画。画面像一部高级艺术科幻电影中的暂停帧。

有限色盘，颜色克制统一，不要杂色；柔和高光，亮部有轻微光晕但不过曝；暗部低对比，不死黑，保留层次；空气中有雾气、水汽、尘埃和光束介质；人物在巨大空间中显得很小，用空间关系制造孤独感和电影感。

变形宽银幕镜头，浅景深，边缘轻微散焦，真实镜头衰减，柔和背景虚化，电影级焦外，轻微胶片颗粒，真实曝光，真实材质反射。画面有前景遮挡、中景人物、远景光源，层次清楚，构图克制，留白大，人物不摆拍，像刚好被摄影机捕捉到的一个安静瞬间。

光线要有来源逻辑：一个主要柔光源，少量环境补光，人物边缘有轻微轮廓光；冷色暗部中带一点暖色高光，银蓝、雾灰、石黑、象牙白、微弱金色组成统一色盘。不要过锐，不要高饱和，不要赛博朋克霓虹，不要概念图感，不要塑料AI质感。
```

## 3. 最关键的电影感模块

### A. 有限色盘

```text
limited cinematic color palette: silver blue, mist gray, stone black, muted ivory, pale cyan, subtle warm amber highlight, controlled saturation, no random colors, no colorful neon, elegant tonal unity
```

```text
有限电影色盘：银蓝、雾灰、石黑、低饱和象牙白、淡青色、极少量暖琥珀高光；颜色统一克制，不出现杂色，不出现彩色霓虹，不高饱和。
```

### B. 柔高光

```text
soft controlled highlights, gentle halation around bright areas, highlight roll-off like film, no blown-out whites, luminous but restrained light
```

```text
柔和受控的高光，亮部有电影胶片般的渐变衰减，微弱光晕，不死白，不过曝，光感高级但克制。
```

### C. 低对比暗部

```text
low-contrast shadows, deep but readable blacks, soft shadow detail, no crushed black, no harsh digital contrast
```

```text
暗部低对比，黑位深但保留细节，不死黑，不硬压暗部，没有数码硬对比。
```

### D. 空气介质

```text
visible atmosphere, volumetric haze, humid air, suspended dust particles, mist catching the light, light beams softened by air
```

```text
空气可见，有体积雾、水汽、悬浮尘埃，光线被空气柔化，远处被雾气轻微吞没。
```

### E. 人物缩小和空间

```text
a small human figure placed within a vast space, extreme scale contrast, negative space, spatial loneliness, architectural silence, the environment dominates the frame
```

```text
人物很小，被放在巨大空间中，空间压过人物；大面积留白，尺度反差强，环境成为情绪主体。
```

### F. 镜头与景深

```text
anamorphic lens, shallow depth of field, soft defocused edges, realistic lens falloff, subtle lens breathing, cinematic bokeh, foreground obstruction, layered depth
```

```text
变形宽银幕镜头，浅景深，边缘轻微散焦，真实镜头衰减，柔和焦外，前景遮挡，中景人物，远景光源，空间层次明确。
```
